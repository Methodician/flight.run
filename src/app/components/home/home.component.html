Projects

The MVP

All great startups begin with a "Minimum Viable Product". Our skills, tools, and technologies allow for rapid prototyping but can be scaled and transitioned to enterprise architecture. Your MVP won't need to be scrapped if you go viral, and you've already got a solid team familiar with your codebase and business model.


We're mastering the art of the MVP.


We've isolated 3 paths from viable idea to initial success:

Meteoric Take-off

Rapid user adoption of a new product or service. Usually best suited for low-margin, mass-market ideas. Helps make your idea presentation ready.

Cash Flow Positive

More money is generated by the product than it consumes. Usually best suited for sales-heavy, high-margin products or services.Helps make your idea presentation ready.

Presentation Ready

The product or idea is compelling to investors and early adopters. Your elevator pitch is legitimate and you have some skin in the game. Ideally, you have some cash flow and/or lots of customers.
